### Design Plan for Retro Film Style Image

**Background**:  
- Gradient from dark brown to deep green, with evenly distributed noise for a vintage film grain effect.  
- Irregularly scattered stars, some with subtle trailing effects to mimic long-exposure photography.  

**Earth and Moon**:  
- Earth rendered in low-resolution with intentionally reduced clarity and desaturated colors (50% original tones) for an aged photograph look. A blurred orange halo surrounds the Earth to simulate reflected sunlight.  
- Moon designed with matte texture, simplified surface details, and gray-brown tones blending harmoniously with the background.  

**Light Trail**:  
- Dashed line connecting Earth and Moon, colored light brown to enhance the retro aesthetic.  

**Composition**:  
- Earth positioned on the left side, slightly off-center, with clear visibility of its desaturated landmasses and oceans.  
- Moon placed higher and to the right, maintaining proportional distance and size relative to Earth.  

**Text (if applicable)**:  
- Serif font (Bodoni), color set to off-white, centered at the top. Increased line spacing to align with the vintage theme. Text language: Spanish.  

**Image Dimensions**:  
- 1920x1080 pixels.  

This design plan ensures a cohesive retro film style that evokes nostalgia while presenting Earth and Moon from a cosmic perspective.
