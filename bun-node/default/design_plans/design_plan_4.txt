### Design Plan for Minimalist Flat Style

**Image Dimensions**: 1080×1920 pixels  
**Language**: Chinese  
**Font**: Alibaba PuHuiTi  

#### Background & Layout
- Use pure white background with a single blue tone as the primary color.
- All elements are designed in flat style, ensuring minimal visual distractions.

#### Top Section: "Traditional vs Large Model"
- Two rectangular boxes placed centrally at the top:
  - Left box (Traditional): Filled with gray, containing simplified linear icons.
  - Right box (Large Model): Filled with blue, also with simplified linear icons.

#### Middle Section: "Five-Stage Framework"
- Five square cards arranged horizontally in the middle section:
  - Each card includes one keyword and its corresponding icon.
  - Cards are evenly distributed with sufficient spacing for clarity.

#### Bottom Section: "Time Allocation + Golden Rule"
- Bar chart positioned close to the bottom edge:
  - Bars labeled with percentages “40%”, “30%”, “20%”, “10%”.
  - Specific values displayed at the end of each bar.
- Below the bar chart, include the golden rule text: “提问驱动学习”.

#### Visual Order & Hierarchy
- Ensure clear information hierarchy from top to bottom.
- Maintain balanced spacing between sections for smooth readability.
- Align all elements centrally or symmetrically to enhance the minimalist aesthetic.
