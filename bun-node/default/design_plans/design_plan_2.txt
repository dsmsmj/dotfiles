Design Plan:  
- **Style**: Geometric Floating Style.  
- **Background**: Light blue gradient with subtle grid texture for enhanced depth.  
- **Top Section**: Symmetrical rectangular modules, left in dark gray (traditional mode + simple icon), right in bright blue (large model mode + complex icon). Text: "传统 vs 大模型".  
- **Middle Section**: Five floating circles with soft shadows, horizontally aligned and connected by arrows. Each circle contains a simplified icon. Text labels for the five-stage framework placed neatly below each circle.  
- **Bottom Section**: Circular pie chart centered at the lower part, displaying proportions "40%," "30%," "20%," "10%" in distinct colors. Three small tags around the chart labeled "提问," "思考," "行动."  
- **Typography**: Chinese language, using Foundertype Lanting Xian Hei font. Minimalist and clear.  
- **Dimensions**: 1080×1920 pixels.  
- **Hierarchy & Flow**: Balanced spacing between sections; top modules centered, middle framework slightly above center, bottom pie chart anchored near the base. Clean alignment and logical flow maintained throughout.
