Design Plan for Minimalist Futuristic Style:

Create a 1920x1080 pixel image featuring a pure black background with precisely arranged small white dots representing stars. Position a simplified vector-style Earth on the left side, rendered in low-saturation gray for land and deep blue for oceans, maintaining clear geometric contours without complex textures. Place a similarly styled moon on the upper right, three times smaller than Earth, in regular gray-white tones.

Connect both celestial bodies with a straight light trail starting from Earth's center to the moon's center in cool light blue, gradually tapering towards the moon for perspective effect. Apply subtle radial gradients around Earth and moon for depth while ensuring all elements align perfectly with grid proportions.

If text is required, use Roboto font in light gray color, increased letter spacing, placed centrally at the bottom with 80% transparency. Ensure all elements maintain strict geometric precision and minimalistic aesthetics throughout the composition.
