Design Plan for "Dreamy Watercolor Style":

Create a 1920x1080 pixel artwork with French text, featuring Earth and Moon from a cosmic perspective. Background showcases deep blue watercolor wash transitioning to purple-red and orange-yellow hues, resembling a celestial sunset. Earth appears semi-transparent with soft, hand-painted contours where land and ocean boundaries blur naturally. Surround Earth with a subtle pinkish-purple halo for a dreamlike effect. Render Moon in abstract hand-drawn style with warm gray tones and textured surface, maintaining artistic contrast against the background. Connect both celestial bodies with irregular, pastel pink or yellow curves mimicking meteor trails. Apply soft edge feathering to both Earth and Moon for seamless background integration. If text is included, use Pacifico handwritten font in light gold color, positioned at bottom-right corner with slight tilt for dynamic effect. Ensure all watercolor transitions are achieved using natural edge bleeding techniques for authentic painterly appearance.
