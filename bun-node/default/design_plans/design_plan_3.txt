### Design Plan for "Natural Ecology Style"

**Image Dimensions**: 1080×1920 pixels  
**Language**: Chinese  
**Font Choice**: Rounded sans-serif or handwritten style for warmth and approachability  

#### **Top Section - Traditional vs Large Model**  
- Two tree silhouettes placed centrally:  
  - Left tree (Traditional): Sparse, bare branches in muted tones.  
  - Right tree (Large Model): Lush, vibrant green with detailed foliage.  
- Minimal text labels integrated into the silhouettes to indicate "传统" and "大模型".  

#### **Middle Section - Five-Stage Framework**  
- A vine growing from left to right across the canvas, symbolizing growth and progression.  
- Each stage represented by a flower or fruit icon containing simple line-drawn symbols (e.g., lightbulb, magnifying glass).  
- Icons spaced evenly along the vine path, connected subtly by thin stems or leaves for flow.  

#### **Bottom Section - Time Allocation + Golden Rules**  
- Line graph centered at the bottom, showing percentages "40%," "30%," "20%," and "10%" marked on each node of the line.  
- Three leaves attached to key points on the line graph, labeled with golden rules: "问", "学", "用".  
- Soft green gradients and organic shapes (like water droplets) subtly decorate the background without overpowering the data.  

#### **Overall Visual Order**  
- Balanced composition with clear hierarchy: Trees at the top, vine framework flowing through the middle, and line graph grounded at the bottom.  
- Natural textures (leaf veins, soft gradients) enhance depth while maintaining an inviting, sustainable aesthetic.  
- Ample white space around elements ensures clarity and focus on core content.
