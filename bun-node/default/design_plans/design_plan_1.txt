### Design Plan for Liquid Tech Style

**Image Dimensions**: 1080×1920 pixels  
**Language**: Chinese  
**Font**: Source Han Sans (modern sans-serif)

#### Background
- Soft blue-green gradient with abstract liquid wave structures to create dynamic visual flow.

#### Top Section ("Traditional vs Large Model")
- Two symmetrical liquid circles with black-and-white line icons: "Book" for traditional and "Data Flow" for large model.
- Minimal text labels in Chinese, positioned above each circle.

#### Middle Section ("Five-Stage Framework")
- Curved path connecting five circular nodes, each containing a simple icon (e.g., lightbulb, magnifying glass).
- Icons spaced evenly along the curve, ensuring smooth visual progression.

#### Bottom Section ("Time Allocation + Golden Rules")
- Bar chart displaying percentages: "40%," "30%," "20%," "10%" with corresponding small icons (clock, question mark, brain).
- Summary phrase below chart: “你是研究者，不是学生” (You are a researcher, not a student).

#### Visual Order and Layout
- Clear vertical hierarchy: top, middle, and bottom sections separated by balanced whitespace.
- Icons and text aligned for optimal readability and aesthetic harmony.  

This plan adheres strictly to the fluidity and futuristic essence of the Liquid Tech style while maintaining clarity and logical structure.
