**Design Plan for "Deep Cosmic Elegance" Style**

Create a 1920x1080 pixel image featuring an immersive cosmic scene with Earth and Moon as central elements. Background transitions from deep blue to black, layered with multi-colored nebula textures (pale purple, cyan, gold) processed with Gaussian blur for depth. Stars vary in density, creating distinct Milky Way clusters and empty spaces.

Position Earth slightly left of center with detailed surface features - warm green landmasses and deep blue oceans, surrounded by subtle aurora glow. Place Moon smaller and dimmer in the upper right quadrant with realistic texture but reduced brightness. Connect both celestial bodies with a translucent silver arc (30% opacity) representing their gravitational link.

Apply radial gradients to Earth and Moon for enhanced dimensionality. If text is included, use white Futura font at 80% opacity, centered at bottom. Ensure all elements align with English language content requirements while maintaining artistic cohesion.
